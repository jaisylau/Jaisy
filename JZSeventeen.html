Seventeen

Seventeen is a boy idol group from Pledis Entertainment, debuting on May 25, 2015. They are known to be self-producing idols as they are able to create their own music and choreography. Woozi is the producer, composer, and lyricist for most of the songs that they produce and Hoshi is the choreographer for all their songs. As you may notice, there aren't seventeen members. But there's an explanation for this. The name "Seventeen" represents the thirteen members, three units, and one heart that they share.
